About CUFSpedia

Movies produced for TCU

  1. N FOR NOOBLORD: Started in 2024 but was cancelled after the first act due to a coup inside the TCU (released December 22nd, 2024)
  2. Airforce anarchy: An animation-based film about the original Union Airforce. (Released January 1st, 2025.)
  3. Crimes of Noobingrad Episode one: A documentary about the Porzsydo Rail Massacre (Released February 5th, 2025)
  4. From TCU with love: Romcom about the dictator and the minister of media. Canceled because it was too freaky. (Trailer premiered April 13th, 2025)
